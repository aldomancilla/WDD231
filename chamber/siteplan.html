Site Pursose

The purpose of this website is to create a dynamic online community that supports business growth in Pinula, Guatemala. The site will feature local business directories, provide information on upcoming events, offer networking opportunities, and provide business development tools. The goal is to serve as a one-stop platform for businesses to connect, grow, and contribute to the local economy.

Target Market

Entrepreneurs, business owners, and community members from Pinula and surrounding areas, along with potential investors or partners interested in the region.

Site Goals

  • Facilitate networking and knowledge-sharing among local businesses.
  • Encourage new business establishments and foster economic development.
  • Strengthen the chamber's credibility as the primary business development resource for the community.

User Personas

  • Local Entrepreneur: Marta, a 30-year-old local caf√© owner, is seeking ways to attract more customers, network with fellow business owners, and access resources to expand her operations.
  • Large Business Manager: Roberto, 45, manages a regional office of a larger retail chain. He's looking for opportunities to engage with the local economy and find potential collaborations with smaller businesses in the area.
  • New to Pinula Resident: Ana, a 28-year-old new arrival to Pinula, wants to explore the local business scene and connect with services, shops, and events in the community.

Scenarios

  • A local business owner browses the website to check out the membership options and learn about benefits such as discounts, events, and business resources.
  • A visitor wants to learn more about upcoming seminars and workshops offered by the chamber and registers online to attend.
  • A potential investor from out of town explores the chamber's site for information on market conditions, business statistics, and economic initiatives in Pinula.

SEO Plan

  • Integrate geo-targeted keywords into site content, blogs, and page titles.
  • Use social media platforms to share chamber events and local business success stories, driving traffic to the website.
  • Collaborate with local media outlets to feature chamber events, ensuring inbound links to the site.
  • Optimize the site for mobile access to improve search engine rankings and user experience.
